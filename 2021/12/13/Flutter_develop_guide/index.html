五、MVVM 设计模式

MVVM设计模式是指：Model + View + ViewModel

Model: use Provider.of, Consumer, Selector to konw the change between data

View: 只负责页面绘制

ViewModel: extends ChangeNotifier and use notifyListener() to notify the widget.
